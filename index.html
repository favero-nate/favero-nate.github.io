--- layout: home title: My website subtitle: --- I am currently an associate professor in the Department of Public Administration & Policy within the School of Public Affairs at American University (Washington, DC). I also regularly serve as a visiting faculty member in the Department of Political Science at Aarhus University (Denmark). I received a PhD in political science from Texas A&M University in 2016. My research interests include citizen satisfaction, performance, management, social equity, quantitative methodology, education policy, and systems-level models of public administration/policy.
